# Micro Spec

> For quick tasks. Takes 30 seconds to fill in. Prevents 30 minutes of rework.

**What:** [one sentence — what are we building or fixing]
**For whom:** [who sees the output]
**Constraints:** [2-3 hard limits]
**Done when:** [one measurable outcome]
**Quality bar:** [premium / polished / quick]

> Challenge: Before building, ask — is there a simpler solution that solves the root problem?
